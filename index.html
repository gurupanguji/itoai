About the Instructor

Arjun Nair, Entrepreneurship Educator & Business Mentor

Arjun Nair is the Co-Founder of Great Learning, a leading global edtech platform. Arjun's entrepreneurial journey is marked by resilience and innovation. After earning an MS from Duke University and an MBA from MIT Sloan—where he was awarded the prestigious Legatum Fellowship—he co-founded Great Learning, which grew into a global leader in professional education with over $100 million in revenue and was acquired by Byju's in a $600 million deal.

Arjun's passion for education stems from personal experiences. Overcoming early academic challenges and the loss of his father at 16, he transformed adversity into a drive to get a world class education and then empower others through learning. His leadership at Great Learning has impacted over a million learners across 140+ countries, emphasizing practical skills and career readiness.

In this summer program, Arjun brings his rich experience to inspire young minds. Participants will benefit from his insights into building impactful ventures, understanding MVPs, and navigating the entrepreneurial landscape.
